About Me !

My name is Krista H. Elium and I love balloons !!

I started The Balloonatic Asylum in 2012 after a semi-successful career as a high-energy physisist. I had decided that I would rather spend time at home with my cats and balloons rather than try to unravel the mysteries of self-sustaining cold fusion.

I'm sure that my parents and friend are thrilled that I spent my life learning physics and decided to toss all of that for the lucrative career of balloon sales !!

When I'm not taking care of my cats and goldfish, I like to take deep breaths of helium and cold call people to renew thier auto warranties
